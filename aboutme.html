ABOUT ME

I'm biotechnologist by profession, but I am still looking for my path. That's why I'm discovering my own talents and opportunities offered by the 21st century. I'm lucky enough to live in a time when universal access to the Internet opens many doors and provides a wealth of knowledge (as well as entertainment). In all this splendor, I discovered a piece of programming for myself, that challenges my analytical skills. Sometimes bring frustration when facing difficulties but even greater satisfaction from solving them. Privately, I'm enjoying the respite among nature (as far as possible from the madness of the 21st century) and among people close and kind to me.
