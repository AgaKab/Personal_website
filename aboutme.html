ABOUT ME

I am a motivated and enthusiastic biotechnologist with a great desire to act and learn. I like working with people from whom I learn a lot and discover new opportunities. However, I strive for independence in performing my tasks and I willingly share my knowledge. Self-education gave me crusial skills in my private and professional life. Recently, I find satisfaction in programming, where the continuity of events gives me a chance to use the potential of logical and analytical thinking. This is my new way to spend time effectively and self-development. Privately, I like to rest among nature (as far as possible from the madness of the 21st century) and people close and kind to me.
